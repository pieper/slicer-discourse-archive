## Post #1 by @PaoloZaffino (2023-01-26 15:58 UTC)

<p>We are looking for a motivated person to work as a post-doc on a medical image analysis project at Magna Graecia University of Catanzaro, Italy.</p>
<p>The project, in collaboration with the European Institute of Oncology (Milan) and Azienda Ospedaliero-Universitaria Cagliari, aims at using CT scans and artificial intelligence to predict the therapeutic outcomes (both pharmacological and surgical) for gynecological cancer patients.</p>
<p>Required qualifications and background:</p>
<ul>
<li>PhD in Biomedical Engineering, Computer Science, Computer Vision, Data Science, Medical Physics, or related field, with emphasis on medical image analysis and artificial intelligence (both machine learning and deep learning)</li>
<li>Proved publication track record (minimum Scopus H-index equal to 7)</li>
<li>Younger than 40 years old</li>
</ul>
<p>Payscale at postdoc level depending on the candidate’s experience.<br>
We offer flexible working time and a combination of local/remote working schemes.</p>
<p>No tenure-track academic position is linked to this job offer.</p>
<p>Position duration is 24 months.</p>
<p>To apply for this position, or for any additional information, email to <a href="mailto:p.zaffino@unicz.it">p.zaffino@unicz.it</a></p>
<p>The deadline for applications is March 10th.</p>

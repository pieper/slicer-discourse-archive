## Post #5 by @lassoan (2024-10-10 04:01 UTC)

<p>It is in the <code>Converters</code> category. It is not very intuitive (having it in <code>Utilities</code> could be just as good or better), but categories do not matter much since we introduced the “Module finder”. I would recommend to always use the “Module finder” to switch between modules by clicking on the search (magnifier) icon in the module selector or hitting Control-F (note: on macOS keyboard “Control” is labeled as ⌘, Cmd, or Command).</p>

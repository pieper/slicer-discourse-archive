## Post #2 by @lassoan (2023-05-10 05:06 UTC)

<p>Slicer is used by tens of thousands of researchers with a wide range of imaging modalities, so any incorrect interpretation of input data would have been reported many times. Therefore, if there are any misalignments then it is much more likely due to incorrect export from LifeX. I would recommend to report the error to LifeX developers. If they think that their outputs are correct then they can provide more details here and we can help with further investigation.</p>

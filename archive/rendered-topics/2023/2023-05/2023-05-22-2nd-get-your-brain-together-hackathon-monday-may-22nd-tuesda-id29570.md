<h3><a name="p-95102-what-2" class="anchor" href="#p-95102-what-2" aria-label="Heading link"></a>What</h3>
<p>The <strong>Get Your Brain Together</strong> hackathons <strong>bring together neuroimage data generators, image registration researchers, and neurodata compute infrastructure providers for a hands-on, collaborative event</strong>. This community collaboration aims to <strong>create reproducible, open source resources</strong> that enable discovery of the structure and function of brains.</p>
<p>There are three components to the hackathon. First, the primary goal of each hackathon is the generation of a <strong>Reproducible Resource</strong> for registration and analysis of a specific brain imaging modality. <strong>Tutorial</strong> sessions share how to work with open source registration tools, open access datasets, or neurodata archives. <strong>Birds-of-a-Feather (BOF) Breakout</strong> sessions enable participants interested in collaborating to work on relevant topics.</p>
<p>This hackathon will focus on registration of <strong>mouse brain lightsheet volumes</strong> to the <a href="https://doi.org/10.1016/j.cell.2020.04.007">Common Coordinate Framework</a> version 3.1 (CCFv3.1).</p>
<p><strong>Example ways to participate:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><a href="https://insightsoftwareconsortium.github.io/GetYourBrainTogether/HCK02_2023_Allen_Institute_Hybrid/#reproducible-resource-challenge">Contribute a registration pipeline resource</a> to register the challenge datasets.</li>
<li><a href="https://insightsoftwareconsortium.github.io/GetYourBrainTogether/HCK02_2023_Allen_Institute_Hybrid/#tutorials">Give a tutorial</a> about your registration tools. A pre-recorded or live presentation, along with example code and recipes, can teach a data analyst how to run your tool and use the output on the challenge dataset and/or another open dataset.</li>
<li><a href="https://insightsoftwareconsortium.github.io/GetYourBrainTogether/HCK02_2023_Allen_Institute_Hybrid/#birds-of-a-feather-breakouts">Come and learn</a> about registration from experts in the field.</li>
</ul>

<h3><a name="p-95102-during-the-hackathon-5" class="anchor" href="#p-95102-during-the-hackathon-5" aria-label="Heading link"></a>During the Hackathon</h3>
<p>The week will start 9 AM Pacific Time, 12 AM Eastern Time, Monday, May 22nd in an introductory all-hands videoconference.</p>
<p>Following the introduction, participate in the Reproducible Resource Challenge, join the tutorials, and take part in BoF breakouts. Please bring a laptop.</p>
<p>At the end of the day on Tuesday, participants will delegate one member to present their registration processing pipelines, results, and discuss lessons learned.</p>

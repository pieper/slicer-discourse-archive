## Post #4 by @pieper (2024-07-18 20:15 UTC)

<p>The error message you posted indicates 10.13 is needed, but you have 10.12.  In general you should get the latest OS version your hardware supports.  We try to support as many configurations as possible, but after about 5 years we typically can’t support older systems.  We don’t track version by version of OS and Slicer so I can’t tell you the minimum you must have.</p>

## Post #2 by @muratmaga (2024-07-11 20:34 UTC)

<p>As long as you can import your confocal stack with correct image spacing, you can measure visualize whatever you want.</p>
<p>If can export your confocal as tiff sequence, everything would be pretty easy if you use the <code>ImageStacks</code> module that comes with the SlicerMorph extension.</p>

<h4><a name="p-114603-autoscoper-3" class="anchor" href="#p-114603-autoscoper-3" aria-label="Heading link"></a>Autoscoper:</h4>
<ul>
<li><strong>Challenge:</strong> 3D to 3D registration with Elastix presents challenges, especially when the fixed volume includes a complete set of structures (e.g., bones) and the moving volume is a partial volume with a single structure.
<ul>
<li><strong>Recommendations:</strong>
<ul>
<li>Roughly crop the fixed volume to include a single structure.</li>
<li>If segmentation of both structures is available, consider using <a href="https://github.com/SlicerRt/SegmentRegistration">SegmentRegistration</a>.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>

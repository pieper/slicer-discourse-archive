## Post #1 by @finetjul (2024-02-27 14:27 UTC)

<p><img src="https://emoji.discourse-cdn.com/twitter/new.png?v=12" title=":new:" class="emoji" alt=":new:" loading="lazy" width="20" height="20"> A new 3D Slicer course is available: <strong>3D Slicer for developers</strong> !</p>
<p><img src="https://emoji.discourse-cdn.com/twitter/teacher.png?v=12" title=":teacher:" class="emoji" alt=":teacher:" loading="lazy" width="20" height="20">On March 18th 2024, Kitware Europe will offer a developer course to give developers a good understanding of the core concepts and tools of Slicer that are needed to start developing custom solutions and extend Slicer capabilities.</p>
<p>For course details and registration: <a href="https://www.kitware.eu/slicer-for-developers-training/" rel="noopener nofollow ugc">https://www.kitware.eu/slicer-for-developers-training/ </a></p>
<p><img src="https://emoji.discourse-cdn.com/twitter/teacher.png?v=12" title=":teacher:" class="emoji" alt=":teacher:" loading="lazy" width="20" height="20">Additionally, <strong>for Slicer users</strong>, Kitware Europe will offer a beginner course to learn about 3D Slicer for your medical imaging needs on March 15th 2024.</p>
<p>The objectives are as follows:</p>
<ol>
<li>Loading and Saving data (DICOM, models,…)</li>
<li>Mastering the 3DSlicer’s interface</li>
<li>Using segmentation and registration algorithms</li>
<li>Introduction to Python scripting</li>
</ol>
<p>For course details and registration: <a href="https://www.kitware.eu/3d-slicer-training/" rel="noopener nofollow ugc">https://www.kitware.eu/3d-slicer-training/ </a></p>
<p>Courses are live only (interactive, no recording), online or on premise in Lyon, France.</p>

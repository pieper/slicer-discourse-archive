<p>Dear colleagues, this message serves to invite you to submit a paper to the upcoming Modeling and Simulation in Medicine Workshop, within the 2025 ANNSIM Modeling &amp; Simulation conference.</p>
<p>2025 ANNUAL MODELING AND SIMULATION CONFERENCE (ANNSIM) - <a href="http://annsim.org" rel="noopener nofollow ugc">annsim.org</a><br>
May 26-29, 2025 Complutense University of Madrid, Spain<br>
MODELING AND SIMULATION IN MEDICINE WORKSHOP<br>
Chairs: Prof. Michel Audette, Old Dominion University<br>
Prof. Jerzy Rozenblit, University of Arizona</p>
<p><strong>Dates</strong><br>
<strong>Paper submission Jan 19, 2025</strong><br>
<strong>Acceptance notification (papers) March 7, 2025</strong><br>
<strong>Camera-ready version March 26, 2025</strong><br>
<strong>Conference Program Announced April 9, 2025</strong></p>
<h1><a name="p-120336-topics-1" class="anchor" href="#p-120336-topics-1" aria-label="Heading link"></a><strong>Topics</strong></h1>
<p>The conference will focus on (but is not limited to) the following topical areas:</p>
<ul>
<li>Modeling and simulation in medicine: fundamental research</li>
<li>Training and education</li>
<li>Care delivery, outcomes, and patient’s safety</li>
<li>Robotics and its applications in training and “in vivo”</li>
<li>Life-critical systems</li>
<li>Systems integration: “connected health”</li>
<li>Cybersecurity and healthcare</li>
<li>Human performance and ergonomics models</li>
<li>Discrete-event and agent-based approaches to healthcare</li>
<li>Physiological modeling, from single-system to integrative approaches</li>
<li>Multi-scale modeling and simulation in medicine</li>
<li>Descriptive anatomical modeling for medical simulation</li>
</ul>

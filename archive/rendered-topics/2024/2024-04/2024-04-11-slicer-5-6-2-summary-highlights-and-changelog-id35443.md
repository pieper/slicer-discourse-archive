<h1 id="heading--summary">Summary</h1>
<p>Slicer 5.6.2 is a patch release published in April 2024, aimed at addressing various issues reported by the community and providing bug fixes, enhancements, and improvements.</p>
<p>For more details and a comprehensive list of changes, enhancements, and highlights in the Slicer 5.6 releases, please also refer to the <a href="https://discourse.slicer.org/t/slicer-5-6-summary-highlights-and-changelog/33140">Slicer 5.6</a> and <a href="https://discourse.slicer.org/t/slicer-5-6-1-summary-highlights-and-changelog/33371">Slicer 5.6.1</a> release notes.</p>
<p><em>Please note that Slicer continues to be a research package and is not intended for clinical use (clinical users must obtain the necessary ethics or regulatory approvals).</em></p>

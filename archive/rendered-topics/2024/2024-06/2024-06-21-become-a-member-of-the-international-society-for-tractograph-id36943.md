## Post #1 by @maxime.descoteaux (2024-06-21 18:35 UTC)

<p>We are thrilled to officially announce the launch of the <a href="http://www.tractography.io" rel="noopener nofollow ugc">International Society for Tractography (IST)</a>. Our goal is to facilitate fundamental advances in the field of tractography that are only possible via an interactive community dedicated to sharing a broad range of talent and knowledge. This includes planning engaging events such as webinars, educational workshops, and conferences, as well as promoting large-scale collaborations across institutions and international borders.</p>
<p>It’s now time to sign up and <a href="http://www.tractography.io/become_a_member/" rel="noopener nofollow ugc">become a member</a>. The first year (July 1st 2024 - June 30th 2025), membership will be free!</p>

## Post #2 by @jmhuie (2024-06-25 21:50 UTC)

<p>Hi Cristian</p>
<p>The easiest way to tell is to look at the slider and values at the top bar of the slice view. 1% will be more to the left and smaller values. 100% will be more to the right and larger values. See the attached photo to get a better idea of the slider and values I’m referring to.</p>

## Post #2 by @diazandr3s (2024-06-17 10:01 UTC)

<p>Hi <a class="mention" href="/u/samuelholly">@samuelholly</a>,</p>
<p>That’s correct. The Whole body segmentation TS1 (v1.0.0) model is based on the SegResNet architecture. This network architecture was first presented here:  <a href="https://arxiv.org/pdf/1810.11654" rel="noopener nofollow ugc"><em>3D MRI brain tumor segmentation using autoencoder regularization</em></a></p>
<p>Otherwise you can refer to the Auto3DSeg reference: <a href="https://docs.monai.io/en/1.3.1/auto3dseg.html" class="inline-onebox" rel="noopener nofollow ugc">Auto3dseg — MONAI 1.3.1 Documentation</a></p>

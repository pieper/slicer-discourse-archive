## Post #2 by @ungi (2024-09-28 12:36 UTC)

<p>Hi, you can show the contents of the 2D view in the 3D view. You need to expand the view controls using the icon that looks like a horizontal pushpin. See “Show slice in 3D views” under “View data” in this page: <a href="https://slicer.readthedocs.io/en/latest/user_guide/getting_started.html#using-slicer" rel="noopener nofollow ugc">Getting Started — 3D Slicer documentation</a><br>
I hope this helps.</p>

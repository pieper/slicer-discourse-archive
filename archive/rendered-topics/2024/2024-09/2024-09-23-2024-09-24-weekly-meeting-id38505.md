<h3><a name="p-117797-meeting-notes-1" class="anchor" href="#p-117797-meeting-notes-1" aria-label="Heading link"></a>Meeting Notes</h3>
<h4><a name="p-117797-support-of-nm-dicom-with-negative-spacing-2" class="anchor" href="#p-117797-support-of-nm-dicom-with-negative-spacing-2" aria-label="Heading link"></a>Support of NM DICOM with negative spacing</h4>
<p>There is an ongoing discussion regarding the handling of negative spacing in Nuclear Medicine (NM) DICOM images, particularly for Single-Photon Emission Computed Tomography (SPECT). A key question is whether the GDCM library should check for SPECT modality and allow negative spacing values.</p>
<p><strong>Short-term plan:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide a detailed summary of the current challenges and ongoing discussions.</li>
<li>Develop a Slicer-specific solution to address the regression before the upcoming Slicer release.</li>
</ul>

## Post #1 by @lassoan (2024-10-13 19:19 UTC)

<p>Dr. Alexandra Golby has been named “Innovator of the Year” at Congress of Neurological Surgeons annual meeting for her groundbreaking work on <a href="https://www.nousnav.org/">NousNav</a>, a low-cost neurosurgical navigation system based on 3D Slicer for developing countries.</p>

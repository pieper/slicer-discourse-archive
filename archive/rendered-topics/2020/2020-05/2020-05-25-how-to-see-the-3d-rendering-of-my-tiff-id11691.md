## Post #2 by @lassoan (2020-05-25 13:29 UTC)

<p>It looks like the CT is not calibrated properly (it is not uncommon for non-clinical scanners), so you need to adjust the “Shift” slider manually to compensate for the incorrect image offset/scaling.</p>

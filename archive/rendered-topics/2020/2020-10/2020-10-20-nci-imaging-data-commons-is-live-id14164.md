## Post #1 by @fedorov (2020-10-20 16:24 UTC)

<p>Today we are happy to announce the public release of the NCI Imaging Data Commons!</p>
<p>Learn more about IDC in this blog post: <a href="https://datascience.cancer.gov/news-events/blog/imaging-data-commons-brings-power-cloud-cancer-research">https://datascience.cancer.gov/news-events/blog/imaging-data-commons-brings-power-cloud-cancer-research</a></p>
<p>The portal is available here: <a href="https://portal.imaging.datacommons.cancer.gov/">https://portal.imaging.datacommons.cancer.gov/</a></p>
<p>This is the pilot release of the resource. We look forward to your feedback to direct our continuing development and refinement of IDC. Please share your feedback on the IDC Discourse forum: <a href="https://discourse.canceridc.dev/">https://discourse.canceridc.dev/</a>.</p>

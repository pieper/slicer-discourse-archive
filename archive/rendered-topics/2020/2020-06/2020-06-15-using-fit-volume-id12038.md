## Post #2 by @lassoan (2020-06-15 18:29 UTC)

<p>The purple cube is only used for placing the orientation letter (L/R, A/P, I/S) and set the center of rotation. Since your model is already nicely in the middle, you can leave the cube as is, but if you want then you can click the small rectangle icon in the view controller, next to the push-pin icon and the view name (“1”) to fit the cube size to the displayed data.</p>

## Post #3 by @lassoan (2020-06-17 14:07 UTC)

<p>Click the <kbd>%</kbd> button next to the size preset buttons to switch from “percentage of screen size” to “millimeter”.</p>
<p>Check “Sphere brush” checkbox to switch from paint with a disk to paint with a sphere.</p>

## Post #2 by @lassoan (2020-12-04 03:05 UTC)

<p>This is caused by a bug in your Intel Graphics driver. Upgrade your driver to the latest version to fix it.</p>
<p>If you are interested in more details, you can <a href="https://discourse.slicer.org/search?q=green%20stripes">search for “green stripes” on this site</a>.</p>

## Post #2 by @lassoan (2020-07-29 22:20 UTC)

<p>The segmentation is visible, you just need to zoom in quite a bit. If you load the master volume and click <a href="https://slicer.readthedocs.io/en/latest/user_guide/user_interface.html#slice-view">“Reset field of view” button in the corner of the slice viewer</a> then you should see both the image and segmentation in the slice viewers, too.</p>
<p>If you still have problems, please send the segmentation file (upload to dropbox/onedrive/gdrive and send the link) or at least send the application log (menu: Help / Report a bug) of a failed loading. Thank you!</p>

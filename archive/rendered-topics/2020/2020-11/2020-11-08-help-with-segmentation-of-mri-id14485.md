## Post #2 by @manjula (2020-11-08 11:05 UTC)

<p>There are many tools available. The way you have mentioned, you first need to segment the extraocular muscles.</p>
<p>There are resources at <a href="https://www.slicer.org/wiki/Documentation/4.10/Training#Segmentation" rel="noopener nofollow ugc">https://www.slicer.org/wiki/Documentation/4.10/Training#Segmentation</a></p>
<p>Also if you google you will find a few more.</p>
<p>Once you have properly segment them you can use segmentation statistics to get the volume and many more parameters if you require so.</p>

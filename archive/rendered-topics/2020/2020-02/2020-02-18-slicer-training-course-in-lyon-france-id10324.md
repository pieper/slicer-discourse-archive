## Post #1 by @finetjul (2020-02-18 06:35 UTC)

<p>Kitware will be holding a Slicer training course on March 20th 2020 in Lyon, France.</p>
<p>This one-day course will cover the Slicer interface, the loading, manipulation, annotation and saving of dataset and an introduction on writing your own modules/plugins in Python.</p>
<p>Please visit our website for more information, registration details and other trainings (ITK, VTK, ParaView…):</p>
<p><a href="https://training.kitware.fr/browse/241/3d-slicer" rel="nofollow noopener">https://training.kitware.fr</a></p>
<p>Note that the course will be taught in English.</p>

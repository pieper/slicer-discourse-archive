## Post #1 by @mikebind (2020-08-14 18:09 UTC)

<p>Using 4.11.0-2020-08-10.  Clicking on the Help menu inside Slicer, then “Report a bug” advises the user to post a new topic to the Slicer forum, or “submit a bug report to the 3D Slicer bugtracker”.  Clicking on the link to the bugtracker leads to a “404 not found” message at <a href="https://www.na-mic.org/Bug/my_view_page.php" rel="nofollow noopener">https://www.na-mic.org/Bug/my_view_page.php</a></p>

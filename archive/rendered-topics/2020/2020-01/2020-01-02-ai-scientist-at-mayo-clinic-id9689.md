<p>Hi all.  We have postings for clinical track AI scientists in the department of radiology at the Mayo Clinic in Rochester, MN USA.  The position would involve working within the radiology department to translate AI algorithms into the clinical practice.  Please don’t hesitate to contact me for information.</p>
<blockquote>
<p>The Division of Informatics in the Department of Radiology at Mayo Clinic, Rochester, MN invites applications for positions in our clinical support team. This position is an associate consultant role of limited term, with possibility for renewal.</p>
</blockquote>
<blockquote>
<p>We are currently in the process of recruiting candidates for the administrative and operational structures required to support discovery to practice. The goal of the initiative is to place Mayo Clinic at the vanguard of the Artificial Intelligence field with Radiology being an early opportunity area.</p>
</blockquote>
<blockquote>
<p>Qualified individuals should possess a Ph.D. in a scientific field with experience in developing and writing advanced computational algorithms. A strong component of machine learning and programming background, along with demonstrated ability to manage all aspects of an experimental research project, including coordination, data acquisition and analysis, software development and documentation. Excellent communication skills and the ability to manage teams of scientists and other healthcare professionals are essential.</p>
</blockquote>
<blockquote>
<p>Specific innovation areas of interest for this position include support for Department of Radiology Artificial Intelligence projects. The successful candidate will join a team charged to facilitate and optimize the discovery, translation and application of artificial intelligence, machine learning and deep learning supporting the mission of Mayo Clinic Radiology.</p>
</blockquote>

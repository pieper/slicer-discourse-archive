<p><strong>Key Highlights</strong>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Compute jobs are <strong>executed on a cluster</strong></li>
<li><strong>PyQtGraph</strong> is used for plotting visualizations</li>
<li><strong>PySide</strong> integrated into Slicer with some custom adaptations (minor “hacks”)</li>
<li>Some underlying models are <strong>closed source</strong> due to <strong>patent protection</strong></li>
<li>A specialized <strong>file format</strong> is used to <strong>easily extract subregions</strong> from datasets</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Web Browser Integration</strong>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Using a <strong>VNC-like approach</strong> to provide remote access through web browsers.</li>
</ul>

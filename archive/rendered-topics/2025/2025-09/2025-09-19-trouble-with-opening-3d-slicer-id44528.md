## Post #2 by @muratmaga (2025-09-19 05:54 UTC)

<p>Application is launching but GUI is not visible. This is often due to graphics driver issues. Windows often updates quite a bit of things after the computer has been offline a while.</p>
<p>Perhaps reinstalling the graphics driver on your system and then see if it works.</p>
<p>Also do you have more than one GPU in your system (eg., an integrated one and a discrete one).</p>

<p>Slicer 5.8.1 has a bug where selecting a volume rendering preset doesn’t result in the selection showing in the combobox. This was fixed in a Slicer preview release beginning in March. Please download latest Slicer Preview from <a href="https://download.slicer.org/" rel="noopener nofollow ugc">https://download.slicer.org/</a> and let us know how it goes for you.</p>

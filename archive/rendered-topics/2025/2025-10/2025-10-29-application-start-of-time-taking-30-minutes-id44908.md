## Post #2 by @lassoan (2025-10-29 02:33 UTC)

<p>The startup time should be 20-30 seconds and not 20-30 minutes. That said, the binaries in the Slicer Preview Releases are not signed or notarized, so macOS may perform lengthy extra checks.  Startup time of a Slicer Stable Release should be much faster. A slow disk and certain extensions may also delay the startup.</p>

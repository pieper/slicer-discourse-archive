## Post #2 by @lassoan (2025-01-22 02:24 UTC)

<p>This feature is provided by the <code>Draw</code> effect (works only on a single slice in 2D, allows freehand and polygon drawing) and <code>Scissors</code> effect (works in 2D - single or multiple slices - and also in 3D).</p>

## Post #1 by @jcfr (2025-01-15 15:16 UTC)

<p>The upcoming <strong>Slicer Hackathon</strong> is an <strong>in-person event</strong> at the <strong>Kitware North Carolina office</strong>. While we expect only Kitwareans to attend in person, remote participants are welcome to join via <strong>Google Meet</strong> (link will be shared with registered attendees).</p>
<p>The main goal of this hackathon is to <strong>collaboratively address issues</strong>, with the expectation that each participant will contribute at least <strong>one pull request</strong> that is either:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Reviewed &amp; integrated</strong>, or</li>
<li><strong>Ready to be reviewed &amp; integrated.</strong></li>
</ul>

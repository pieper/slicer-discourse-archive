## Post #2 by @muratmaga (2025-11-10 05:01 UTC)

<p>Why do you want to remove it? If your goal is to retest with new updates, just hit the Reload button (afetr you enable Developer mode).</p>
<p>If you want to remove, go to Module paths under the application settings, remove the path to your module and restart slicer ( as far as I know you cannot unload a module without restarting).</p>

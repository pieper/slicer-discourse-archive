## Post #1 by @Nick_Bearup (2025-11-25 18:56 UTC)

<p>The 3D Slicer community has been working hard toward the release of 3D Slicer 5.10, which was launched earlier this month, and we are very excited about the new features in this major release. You can learn more by attending Kitware’s upcoming 3D Slicer 5.10 release webinar on December 10th, from 12:00pm - 1:00pm ET. The webinar is free, but <a href="https://www.kitware.com/webinars/3d-slicer-5-10-new-workflows-tools-and-core-upgrades/#register" rel="noopener nofollow ugc">make sure you register beforehand</a>.</p>
<p>This release introduces meaningful enhancements designed to help you work more efficiently, build more advanced workflows, and take advantage of the next generation of Python-based tooling.</p>
<p>This webinar will walk through improvements in:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Volume rendering</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>DICOM data handling</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Segmentation workflows</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The addition of new core modules</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Plus much more!</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>More information on the webinar can be found at this link: <a href="https://www.kitware.com/webinars/3d-slicer-5-10-new-workflows-tools-and-core-upgrades/" rel="noopener nofollow ugc">https://www.kitware.com/webinars/3d-slicer-5-10-new-workflows-tools-and-core-upgrades/</a></p>
<p>If you have any questions on the new release, and how it will affect your workflows, please contact Kitware at <a href="mailto:kitware@kitware.com">kitware@kitware.com</a> to schedule a time to meet with the 3D Slicer team.</p>
<p>Hope to see you at the webinar!</p>

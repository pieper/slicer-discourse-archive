## Post #1 by @jcfr (2025-03-02 22:59 UTC)

<p>As discussed in the <a href="https://discourse.slicer.org/t/2025-02-25-weekly-meeting/41873/4">February 25th weekly meeting</a>, the regular <code>Preview</code> and <code>Stable</code> builds of Slicer and its extensions will be temporarily disabled this evening (March 2nd) to facilitate the <strong>Slicer 5.8.1 patch release</strong>.</p>
<p>To track progress and review the integrated fixes, see the <a href="https://github.com/Slicer/Slicer/issues/8258">Slicer 5.8.1 Release Issue</a>.</p>

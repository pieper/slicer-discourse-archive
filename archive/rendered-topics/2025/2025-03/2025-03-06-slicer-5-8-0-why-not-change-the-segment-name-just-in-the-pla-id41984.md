## Post #2 by @cpinter (2025-03-06 15:00 UTC)

<p>We are moving towards a more comprehensive experience with terminologies, which includes a lot of improvements around terminologies (like what you noticed, that now the name editing also pops up the terminology editor), but we also allow turning off terminologies altogether in Application Settings.</p>
<p>From your screenshot I see that you probably would benefit from keeping using terminologies. By showing the selector on editing the segment name, we further encourage using terminologies instead of free text (for a more standard identification of segments, to avoid typos and thus AI model training problems, etc.).</p>
<p>Can you explain why this is a problem for you?</p>

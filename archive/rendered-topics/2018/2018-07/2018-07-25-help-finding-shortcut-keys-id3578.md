## Post #1 by @Charles_Forsythe1 (2018-07-25 20:54 UTC)

<p>Does anyone know the shortcut keys for these:</p>
<ol>
<li>How to turn on/off the ‘eye’ icon next to the segments in ‘segment editor’?</li>
<li>How to fill in the space when you paint a circle?</li>
<li>Shortcut for the ‘Four Up’ key?</li>
</ol>
<p>Here are some shortcut keys I have found:</p>
<ol>
<li>‘W’ let’s you go forward on segments when painting/erasing</li>
<li>‘Q’ let’s you go backwards on segments when painting/erasing<br>
3.‘1’ turns on the paint</li>
<li>‘3’ turns on the eraser</li>
</ol>

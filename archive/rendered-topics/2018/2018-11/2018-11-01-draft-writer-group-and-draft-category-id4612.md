## Post #1 by @jcfr (2018-11-01 01:26 UTC)

<p>A new category named <code>Draft</code> visible only to member of the group <a href="https://discourse.slicer.org/groups/draft-writer">draft-writer</a> was created.</p>
<p>It allows to leverage the <a href="https://meta.discourse.org/t/shared-drafts-allow-staff-to-collaborate-on-posts-before-publishing/83493">shared draft</a> feature of Discourse and is used to draft posts before publicly releasing them. (e.g Release Announcement)</p>

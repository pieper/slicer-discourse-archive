## Post #2 by @lassoan (2018-11-11 13:07 UTC)

<p>Uninstall and reinstall the extension. Make sure to wait for “Restart” button in the extension manager and click that (not the “Close” button"). If Restart button is enabled then it indicates that download and installation of the extension is complete.</p>

## Post #2 by @lassoan (2018-11-15 01:08 UTC)

<p>You can either place annotation rulers; or place markup fiducial points and see distance by right-clicking in the point list in Markups module.</p>
<p>Probably the simplest way to delete annotations and markups is to go to Data module, right-click on what you want to remove, and choose Delete.</p>

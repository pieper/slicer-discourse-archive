## Post #2 by @lassoan (2018-12-17 03:00 UTC)

<p>If you enable showing image slices in 3D, do you see the image slices? If not, then probably just the view direction is net set correctly - click the crosshair button on the virtual reality toolbar (the one next to the headset button).</p>

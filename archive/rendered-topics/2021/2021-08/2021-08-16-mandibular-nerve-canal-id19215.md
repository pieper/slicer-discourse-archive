## Post #7 by @lassoan (2021-08-23 00:43 UTC)

<p>The shortcut is Control + Alt + Left-click-and drag. You do not just click (depress and release) rh button, but hold down the mouse button while you move the pointer.</p>
<p>You also need to make sure slice intersections are displayed (without seeing the slice intersections you cannot rotate them).</p>
<p>On Mac keyboards, Control and Command buttons are swapped (not just for Slicer but for all multi-platform software) so there you need to use Command + Alt + Left-click-and-drag. You can also use pinch-and-rotate gesture on the Mac touchpad to rotate slice intersections.</p>

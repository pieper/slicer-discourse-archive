## Post #2 by @lassoan (2021-08-03 12:04 UTC)

<p>Mimics image is simply rendered at higher resolution. Screen capture is performed at current size and resolution of the view. To increase that, you can switch the layout to “One-up 3D”, maximize your application window size, and increase your screen resolution.</p>
<p>You can make the images look nicer than Mimics by using “Lights” module (in Sandbox extension) by improving lighting and adding shadows. You can also adjust material properties in Models module (Display / 3D Display / Advanced section).</p>

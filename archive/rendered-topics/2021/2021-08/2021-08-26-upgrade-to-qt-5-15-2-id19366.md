## Post #1 by @fbordignon (2021-08-26 14:52 UTC)

<p>Hello everyone, I would like to suggest the upgrade of the Qt version to Qt 5.15.2, as there is a minor bug that is bothering me profoundly. (ok a bit of exaggeration here, but it is annoying)<br>
Buttons with padding show a visual feedback of a click on the padding area but does not emit the clicked signal:<br>
<a href="https://bugreports.qt.io/browse/QTBUG-86587" class="onebox" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow ugc">https://bugreports.qt.io/browse/QTBUG-86587</a><br>
<a href="https://bugreports.qt.io/browse/QTBUG-88276" class="onebox" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow ugc">https://bugreports.qt.io/browse/QTBUG-88276</a></p>
<p>I’ve successfully built and used Slicer with Qt 5.15.2 where the issue is solved. 5.15.2 is the latest open source version available. Do we have any reasons not to upgrade?</p>

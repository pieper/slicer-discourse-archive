## Post #2 by @lassoan (2021-06-06 19:17 UTC)

<p>You can adjust opacity of the entire segmentation and separately for each segment in Segmentations module.</p>
<p>If you just want to quickly change overall opacity then you can right-click on the eye icon in Data module and move the opacity slider.</p>

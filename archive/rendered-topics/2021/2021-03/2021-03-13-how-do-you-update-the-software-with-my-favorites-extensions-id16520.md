## Post #2 by @lassoan (2021-03-13 13:58 UTC)

<p>Application settings are shared between all Slicer versions. Extensions that were installed in previous Slicer versions are listed in the Restore tab in Extensions manager and can be all reinstalled with a few clicks.</p>

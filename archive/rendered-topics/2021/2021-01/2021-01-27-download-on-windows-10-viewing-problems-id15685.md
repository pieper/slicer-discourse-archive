## Post #2 by @lassoan (2021-01-27 02:55 UTC)

<p>This is due to an Intel display driver bug, which breaks a wide range of 3D graphics applications. The solution is to update your graphics driver.</p>
<p>If you are interested, you can find more details about the error here: <a href="https://discourse.slicer.org/t/slicer-opening-screen-error-green-stripes/12543/16">Slicer opening screen error - Green Stripes</a></p>

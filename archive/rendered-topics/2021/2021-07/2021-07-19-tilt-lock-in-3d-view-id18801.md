## Post #1 by @rbumm (2021-07-19 17:40 UTC)

<p>Segmentations can sometimes “tumble” in 3D view.</p>
<p>By pressing Ctrl + “b” in 3D view, you can lock the rotation of the model to the azimuth axis only (available in 4.13 nightly builds). This is a toggle switch.</p>

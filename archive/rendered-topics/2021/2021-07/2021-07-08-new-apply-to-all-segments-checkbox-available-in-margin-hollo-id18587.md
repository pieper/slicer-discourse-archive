# New "Apply to all segments" checkbox available in "Margin", "Hollow", "Smoothing" and "Scissors" effect

**Topic ID**: 18587
**Date**: 2021-07-08
**URL**: https://discourse.slicer.org/t/new-apply-to-all-segments-checkbox-available-in-margin-hollow-smoothing-and-scissors-effect/18587

---

## Post #1 by @rbumm (2021-07-08 16:45 UTC)

<p>As a result of ideas born during the 35th Project Week,  a new "Apply to all segment checkbox was implemented in the  “Margin”, “Hollow”, “Smoothing”, and “Scissors” effects of Slicer’s Segment Editor.</p>
<p><img src="https://us1.discourse-cdn.com/flex002/uploads/slicer/original/3X/f/e/fee736768b8a63732246c2db15d90cc1c65b38a0.png" alt="image" data-base62-sha1="AmYEcU7dhV06butG90IE5KO7WGA" width="273" height="58"></p>
<p>By checking this box, you can initiate easy and quick serial processing of all visible segmentations within a segmentation node.</p>
<p>I created a short video on how to use this with the “Margin” effect in the vertebral body segmentation. Some other videos might follow.</p>
<p>Thank you <a class="mention" href="/u/lassoan">@lassoan</a> for your help.<br>
The functionality is available in release 4.13.xx (currently preview)</p>

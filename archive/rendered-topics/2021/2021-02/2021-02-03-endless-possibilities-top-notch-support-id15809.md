## Post #1 by @rbumm (2021-02-03 10:14 UTC)

<p>We discovered 3D Slicer during our search for a versatile tool for 3D reconstructions of lung CT scans.</p>
<p>3D Slicer proved very helpful for teaching and planning lung surgery as well as during scientific data analysis. The software is complex and powerful, expect a learning curve, but help is always at hand in the vivid Slicer Forum. All interfaces are accessible via Python or C++.</p>

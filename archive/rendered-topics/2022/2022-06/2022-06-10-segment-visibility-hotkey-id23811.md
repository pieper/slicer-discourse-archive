## Post #2 by @lassoan (2022-06-10 15:03 UTC)

<p>Common keyboard shortcuts are listed <a href="https://slicer.readthedocs.io/en/latest/user_guide/user_interface.html#slice-views">here</a>. According to this, <kbd>g</kbd> key toggles segmentation visibility.</p>
<p>Segment Editor shortcuts are listed <a href="https://slicer.readthedocs.io/en/latest/user_guide/modules/segmenteditor.html#keyboard-shortcuts">here</a>.</p>
<p>You can also add any keyboard shortcut for any operation in Slicer. See examples in the <a href="https://slicer.readthedocs.io/en/latest/developer_guide/script_repository.html#keyboard-shortcuts-and-mouse-gestures">script repository</a>. If you have trouble figuring out how to perform the operation that you want to perform with the shortcut then have a look at other examples in the script repository or ask us here.</p>

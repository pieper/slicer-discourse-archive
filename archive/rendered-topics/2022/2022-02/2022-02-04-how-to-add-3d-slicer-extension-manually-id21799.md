## Post #4 by @jamesobutler (2022-02-04 13:19 UTC)

<p>From <a href="https://slicer.readthedocs.io/en/latest/user_guide/extensions_manager.html#install-downloaded-extension-packages" class="inline-onebox" rel="noopener nofollow ugc">Extensions Manager — 3D Slicer documentation</a>,</p>
<blockquote>
<p>Select the the previously downloaded extension package.</p>
</blockquote>
<p>^ This is referring to the download ZIP file. So you should select the ZIP and press “Open” as shown in your image.</p>

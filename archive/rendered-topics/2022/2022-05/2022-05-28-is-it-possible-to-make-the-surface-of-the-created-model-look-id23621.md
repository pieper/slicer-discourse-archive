## Post #2 by @pieper (2022-05-28 15:07 UTC)

<p>Slicer is designed to make the segmentations as faithful a possible to the imaging data (i.e. in size and shape).  To make the model look more like a real bone I suggest you use a visual effects too like <a href="https://www.blender.org/">Blender</a>.</p>

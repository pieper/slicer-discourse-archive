## Post #2 by @muratmaga (2022-03-29 21:49 UTC)

<p>This is very easy, just use the <strong>Logical Operations</strong> effect in segment editor.<br>
Create a one segment for everything, one for your implant, and use Logical Operations to subtract one from the other.<br>
Are you looking for something else?</p>

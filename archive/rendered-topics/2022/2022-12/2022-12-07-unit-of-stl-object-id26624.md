## Post #2 by @lassoan (2022-12-07 12:39 UTC)

<p>Unless you change the sizing scale when you export the STL file, the length unit in the STL file is the same as the length unit in the scene. The length unit in the scene is millimeter by default. All clinical images use millimeter as length unit, too.</p>

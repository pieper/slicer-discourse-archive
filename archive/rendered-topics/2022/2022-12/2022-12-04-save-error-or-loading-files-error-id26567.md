## Post #3 by @lassoan (2022-12-04 05:58 UTC)

<p>Are you trying to load and save a scene in Slicer-5.2 that was created with a much earlier Slicer version?</p>
<p>There can be minor differences between the scene file formats, which may lead to the error messages you reported, but as long as they don’t impact any data files, just some “Scene View” png files (2022-12-04-Scene.png) it is safe to ignore those. To start with a clean slate (and avoid seeing the error messages) you may consider loading just the data files (images, segmentations, markups, etc. - not the .mrml file) in the latest version of Slicer.</p>

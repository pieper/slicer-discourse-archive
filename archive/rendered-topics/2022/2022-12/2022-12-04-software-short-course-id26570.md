## Post #2 by @muratmaga (2022-12-05 05:19 UTC)

<p>I am not aware of a short course that is available currently.</p>
<p>We used to do one that emphasized how to use 3D Slicer for collecting morphometric data from microCT scans of biological specimens, before our funding has ended. You may want to look at contents from Day 1 (general intro to Slicer) and Day 2 (segmentation, second half of the day). If you are not working with microCT data or not interested in morphometrics, skip the references/link to SlicerMorph or SlicerMorphCloud.</p>

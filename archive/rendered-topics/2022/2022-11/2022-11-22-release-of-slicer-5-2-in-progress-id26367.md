## Post #3 by @jcfr (2022-11-24 03:11 UTC)

<p>This evening, regular preview and stable builds of Slicer and associated extensions will be disabled in favor of a <strong>patch</strong> release.</p>
<p>Fixes to be integrated are listed in the <a href="https://github.com/Slicer/Slicer/milestone/7?closed=1">Slicer 5.2.1</a> milestone.</p>
<p>To track the progress, see <a href="https://github.com/Slicer/Slicer/issues/6700" class="inline-onebox">Patch Release Slicer v5.2.1 · Issue #6700 · Slicer/Slicer · GitHub</a></p>
<p>Notes:</p>
<ul>
<li>Thanks <a class="mention" href="/u/lassoan">@lassoan</a> for identifying and fixing the regression <img src="https://emoji.discourse-cdn.com/twitter/pray.png?v=12" title=":pray:" class="emoji" alt=":pray:" loading="lazy" width="20" height="20">
</li>
<li>To avoid similar regressions, issue <a href="https://github.com/Slicer/Slicer/issues/6696">#6696</a> has been created.</li>
</ul>

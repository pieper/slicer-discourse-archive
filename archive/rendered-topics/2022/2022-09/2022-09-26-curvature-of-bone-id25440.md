## Post #10 by @lassoan (2022-09-30 19:32 UTC)

<p>You can choose to constrain the curve to the surface (“Curve settings” section in Markups module).</p>
<p>To improve robustness and accuracy of the measurement, you may make the bone surface smoother, but adjusting the threshold and/or using the Smoothing effect in Segment Editor module.</p>

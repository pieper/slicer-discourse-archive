## Post #2 by @NWoolridge (2022-09-10 21:30 UTC)

<ul>
<li>Go to the Segment Editor.</li>
<li>Click on “Add”.</li>
<li>Click on the “Threshold icon”, and adjust the values to encompass the bone (I used “700” as the low end)</li>
<li>Click Apply</li>
<li>Click on “Show 3D”</li>
<li>If the model that shows up looks good, Click on the tiny downward pointing triangle next to the green arrow icon and choose “Export to files”<br>

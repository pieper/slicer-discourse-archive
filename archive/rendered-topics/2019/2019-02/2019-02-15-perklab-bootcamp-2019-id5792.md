## Post #1 by @cpinter (2019-02-15 16:01 UTC)

<p>The Laboratory of Percutaneous Surgery at Queen’s University is organizing the annual PerkLab bootcamp. It will take place between May 1 and 3 in Kingston, ON, Canada.</p>
<p>This three day course offers a lecture series and hands-on sessions. Although the agenda contains a lab-specific introduction, it is mainly focused on 3D Slicer usage and programming, and image-guided therapy basics. Attendance itself is free of charge.</p>

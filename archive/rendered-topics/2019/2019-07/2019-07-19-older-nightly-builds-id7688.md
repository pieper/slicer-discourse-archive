## Post #3 by @lassoan (2019-07-19 20:50 UTC)

<p>You can also download Slicer releases by specifying day offset or date on the download site - see <a href="https://www.slicer.org/wiki/Documentation/Nightly/FAQ/General#Where_can_I_download_Slicer.3F" rel="nofollow noopener">https://www.slicer.org/wiki/Documentation/Nightly/FAQ/General#Where_can_I_download_Slicer.3F</a></p>

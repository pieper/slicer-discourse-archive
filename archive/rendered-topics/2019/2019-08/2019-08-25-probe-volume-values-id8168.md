## Post #2 by @JanWitowski (2019-08-25 13:15 UTC)

<p>As <a href="https://www.slicer.org/wiki/Documentation/4.10/Modules/DataProbe" rel="nofollow noopener">described in documentation</a>, information highlighted by you are: in the first line patient-space location (R/A/S; Right/Anterior/Superior), spacing between slices for slice view; in the final line value of the volume at current location (usually a Hounsfield Unit value).</p>

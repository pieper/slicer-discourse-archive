<p>Slicer works with any OpenVR (SteamVR) compatible headset.</p>
<p>Probably you can hook up your Oculus Go with SteamVR (with adapters such as <a href="https://www.windowscentral.com/how-play-steamvr-games-oculus-go" rel="nofollow noopener">ALVR</a>), but the experience will be extremely poor/unusable.</p>

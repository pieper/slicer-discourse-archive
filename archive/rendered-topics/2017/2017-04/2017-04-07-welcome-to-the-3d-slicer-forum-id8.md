<p>Welcome to the Slicer forum!</p>
<p><a href="https://www.slicer.org">3D Slicer</a> (“Slicer”) is an open source, extensible software platform for image visualization and analysis. Slicer has a large community of users in medical imaging and surgical navigation, and is also used in fields such as astronomy, paleontology, and 3D printing.</p>

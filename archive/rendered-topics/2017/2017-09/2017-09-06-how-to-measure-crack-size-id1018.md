## Post #2 by @lassoan (2017-09-06 22:55 UTC)

<p>Yes. You can measure distances using annotation ruler tool. Select “Ruler” from the annotation toolbar and click on two points in the image. See more information here: <a href="https://www.slicer.org/wiki/Documentation/Nightly/Modules/Annotations">https://www.slicer.org/wiki/Documentation/Nightly/Modules/Annotations</a></p>

## Post #5 by @lassoan (2017-09-26 18:22 UTC)

<p>Select “Ruler” on the place toolbar before you click on the image:</p>
<p><img src="https://us1.discourse-cdn.com/flex002/uploads/slicer/original/3X/2/9/29579588a68a7d935a6716f9105636994056e80f.png" alt="image" data-base62-sha1="5TJdUyYekZcW9OMBl7OYhxarWGj" width="192" height="175"></p>
<p>That will create rulers when you click on the image. You cannot change the annotation type after it is placed.</p>

## Post #4 by @dzenanz (2017-05-03 12:54 UTC)

<p>I don’t think there is something wrong with Chrome. It is only that Mantis<br>
has really short session validity, and if you take your sweet time to<br>
collect the data after you have opened the form, session can time out.<br>
Result: when you click submit, you get that screen.</p>
<p>Workaround: click back, open new tab, open the bug/note form there, and<br>
copy-paste content from old tab (with expired session).</p>

## Post #4 by @lassoan (2017-12-17 21:16 UTC)

<p>“Center view” will center everything that is currently visible. You have two distant objects shown. If you want to center the view around only one of them:</p>
<ul>
<li>go to Data module</li>
<li>hide the other object</li>
<li>click “Center view”.</li>
</ul>

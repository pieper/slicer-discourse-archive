## Post #2 by @ebrahim (2026-07-16 12:13 UTC)

<p>Nightly builds have been re-enabled. Linux and windows were re-enabled last night before the nightly job started, so they should already be back to normal. macOS was taking its time as usual and was not re-enabled until this morning, so its nightly build dashboard should be back to normal tomorrow.</p>

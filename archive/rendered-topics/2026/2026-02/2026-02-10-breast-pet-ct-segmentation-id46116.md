## Post #2 by @pieper (2026-02-10 21:16 UTC)

<p>Hi and welcome - you can definitely do that work in 3D Slicer.  A good strategy would be to review the tutorials (start at <a href="http://slicer.org">slicer.org</a>) and get familiar with the basic visualization options of the software using the sample data. Then you can try loading and viewing your data.  You can then try some of the segmentation and quantification tutorials and tools.  Be sure to leverage the latest generations of chatbots, as they can be very good at helping you understand the program and documentation.  This forum is good for getting help with specific questions, which are usually possible for us to answer if you provide some screenshots to indicate what you have tried, what you expected, and what you got instead.</p>

<p>Hi James!</p>
<p>Both your ears are confirmed in the scan at great resolution. However I want to be upfront that CBCT shows bone better than soft tissue. The earplug canal fit depends on soft tissue which may not be perfectly captured. The STL may work but I can’t guarantee the companies will accept it.</p>
<p>Just wanted to check, have you looked into flight pressure earplugs like EarPlanes ? They’re specifically designed for pressure regulation during flights and are available immediately in pharmacies. Curious why you’re going the custom route,</p>
<p>is there a specific reason standard flight earplugs won’t work for your condition?</p>
<p>Just want to make sure we get you the best solution in time!</p>

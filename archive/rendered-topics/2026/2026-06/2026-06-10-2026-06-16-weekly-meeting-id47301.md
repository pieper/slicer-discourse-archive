<p>Actually we can plan for a 5.12.1 shortly after. It is more important to have <em>a</em> stable release out before we start breaking things, so maybe we kick some of the issues to the patch.</p>
<p><a href="https://github.com/Slicer/Slicer/issues/9018">#9018: Model turns black under transformation containing reflection</a> actually needs more discussion. When working with transforms, the black model effect is a good suggestion that you may have accidentally done an orientation-reversing transform. Auto-fixing normals could hide one of the best ways of catching certain kinds of mistakes like this. Instead we should consider adding a flip normals or auto-normals feature that can be explicitly invoked.</p>
<p><a href="https://github.com/Slicer/Slicer/pull/9175">#9175: Preserve markups plane normal display settings</a> we might include. Maybe Sam will take it on while waiting for other stuff to get done.</p>
